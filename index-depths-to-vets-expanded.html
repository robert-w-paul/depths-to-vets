Special Monthly Compensation (SMC)

SMC is awarded for severe disabilities and loss of function. This includes tiers like SMC-K through SMC-T. Explore rates and qualification tiers:

TDIU & P&T

Total Disability based on Individual Unemployability (TDIU) provides 100% pay to veterans unable to work due to service-connected disabilities.

Forms:

  • VA Form 21-8940 (TDIU Application)
  • VA Form 21-4192 (Employer Statement)
